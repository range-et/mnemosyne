How to print this tag

Single extruder + paint fill
Print with "Invert modules" OFF. Fill recessed cells with nail polish or acrylic paint. Wipe surface clean when dry.

Two-color / AMS printer
Toggle "Invert modules" to match your filament sequence for color 1 (base) vs color 2 (cells).

Slicer tips
Flat base face on bed. Layer height: 0.15–0.20 mm. No supports needed.
